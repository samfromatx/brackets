Smarter Test Systems In Your Industry

AUTOMOTIVE

Smart vehicles require smarter test systems able to emulate and test ADAS, V2X, infotainment, powertrain, and body and chassis hardware and software as integrated systems.

WIRELESS

New wireless standards such as 802.11ax and 5G, in addition to counter rising costs, manufacturers require smarter test systems optimized for measurement speed and efficiency.

SEMICONDUCTOR

Semiconductor test engineers need smarter test solutions that address cost, design, and device challenges associated with the latest analog, mixed-signal, and RF ICs.

ELECTRONICS

Engineers must create automotive test systems that can scale to avoid obsolescence and support technology updates. This approach ensures test systems that last for decades.

AEROSPACE

Engineers must create smarter aerospace and defense test systems that can scale to avoid obsolescence events and support technology updates, ensuring system longevity.
